◆ CLASSIFIED — MISSION BRIEFING

THE BRIEFING

The enemy fleet lurks somewhere in those waters. Your sonar has detected their presence, but their exact positions remain unknown. Somewhere in that 10×10 grid of grey sea and silence, five ships wait — a Carrier, a Battleship, a Cruiser, a Submarine, a Destroyer. Your orders are simple: find them. Sink them. All of them.

Deploy your own fleet with care — placement is strategy. Then fire your first shot into the fog. A ping. A splash. A hit. Each sunken enemy ship delivers a super weapon into your arsenal — a Carpet Bomb, a Barrage, a Sonar Sweep, a Torpedo Spread, a Precision Strike. Use them wisely. The AI opponent thinks. It hunts. It adapts.

Every battle writes your record. Hit percentages, win streaks, medals earned mid-fight — they all accumulate in your career file. Rise from Cadet to Fleet Admiral. Prove your command is flawless. The ocean doesn't forgive hesitation. Neither does the enemy.

◆ FIELD INTELLIGENCE — COMBAT FEEDBACK SYSTEMS

SENSES OF WAR

💥
HIT FLASH
A white burst erupts on hit cells and fades over 500ms — the visual confirmation of a direct strike.
🔴
SUNK GLOW
Sunk ship cells pulse with a red glow animation — infinite, relentless, marking your victories in the grid.
🟠
AI TURN INDICATOR
Amber pulse blinks while the AI calculates its next attack — time slows when the enemy thinks.
🔊
PCM HIT SOUND
Two-tone impact: 1800Hz dropping to 1000Hz. Synthesized in code — no audio files shipped with the app.
💧
PCM MISS SOUND
Descending sweep from 500Hz down to 150Hz — the hollow sound of a shell vanishing into the deep.
💀
PCM SUNK SOUND
Explosive bass rumble layered with noise — the unmistakable sound of a ship breaking apart.
🎺
PCM WIN FANFARE
Ascending victory fanfare — a C-E-G-C chord sequence rising to mark your command of the seas.
📡
PCM SONAR PING
A sharp 2200Hz blip fires when you select a super weapon — the sonar saying: target acquired.
